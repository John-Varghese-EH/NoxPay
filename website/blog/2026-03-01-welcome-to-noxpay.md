We are excited to announce the official launch of **NoxPay**, a self-hosted, sovereign payment gateway designed for merchants who value privacy, control, and efficiency.

<!--truncate-->

## Why NoxPay?

In a world of centralized payment processors with high fees and opaque rules, NoxPay offers a different path. By hosting your own gateway, you eliminate the middleman and regain control over your financial infrastructure.

### Key Features:

- **Real-time UPI Settlement**: No more waiting for days. Our IMAP-based worker verifies UPI payments instantly by listening to your bank alerts.
- **Crypto Observability**: Native support for Solana (USDC/SOL) and Polygon (USDT) with automated on-chain verification.
- **Privacy-First**: Your data stays on your server. We don't track your customers or your volume.
- **Developer Friendly**: A robust API, Next.js dashboard, and comprehensive documentation to get you started in minutes.

## Our Vision

NoxPay isn't just a tool; it's a statement on financial sovereignty. Whether you're a small business in India looking for reliable UPI automation or a global merchant accepting crypto, NoxPay provides the robust foundation you need.
