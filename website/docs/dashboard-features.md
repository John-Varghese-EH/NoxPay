# Merchant Dashboard

The NoxPay Dashboard is a powerful, no-code interface where you can manage your payments, projects, and security settings.

## 🏠 Project Overview
- **Real-time Analytics**: Track your total volume, success rate, and active payment intents.
- **Transaction History**: Search and filter through all your UPI and Crypto settlements.

## 🔗 No-Code Payment Links
You can generate professional payment links directly from the dashboard:
1.  Click on **"Create Payment Link"**.
2.  Enter the **Amount** and **Description**.
3.  Select which payment methods you want to enable (UPI, USDT, etc.).
4.  Copy the generated URL and share it with your customers via WhatsApp, Email, or Social Media.

## ⚙️ Merchant Settings
- **API Keys**: Manage your `Client ID` and `Client Secret` (sk_live).
- **Webhook Configuration**: Set your `Webhook URL` and rotate your `Webhook Secret`.
- **IP Whitelisting**: Add authorized server IPs to restrict API access.

## 🎨 Checkout Customization
- **Theme Selection**: Toggle between **Dark Mode** and **Light Mode** for your checkout pages.
- **Language Support**: Enable or disable multi-language support (English/Hindi) to cater to your specific customer base.
